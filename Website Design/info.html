Where to find inputs and who built this tool.

Where to Find Input Data

  • Soil pH — Use state/USDA soil maps or local geotech reports; valid range 0–14.
  • Electrical Conductivity — Soil survey data or site sampling; value > 0.
  • Drainage Class — USDA/NRCS classifications (e.g., Well Drained, Poorly Drained).
  • Flood Frequency — Local flood studies or hydrologic records (None → Frequent).

Acceptable ranges and enumerations mirror the project requirements and UI constraints.

How Results Are Presented

The platform provides a 1–5 condition rating with a probability distribution and short factor explanations to support decision-making and documentation. (Export to PDF is planned.)
