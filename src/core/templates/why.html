Why This Project

Purpose, public safety impact, and cost-effective inspection planning.

Purpose

Agencies manage large inventories of culverts with limited budgets. A consistent, explainable, and fast screening tool helps triage structures and standardize communications.

Impact on Public Safety

By highlighting higher-risk candidates sooner, the platform supports proactive maintenance and reduces the chance of failures affecting roads and communities. The 1–5 rating and probability view make risk easier to communicate.

Cost-Effective Inspection Planning

Using lightweight, browser-based inputs, planners can prioritize inspections and allocate crews where the expected benefit is highest—without heavy workflows or new logins.

Design Notes

The UI emphasizes clarity and speed with clean pages, consistent layout, and simple guidance. Export to PDF supports recordkeeping and sharing of results.
